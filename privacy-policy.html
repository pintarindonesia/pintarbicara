Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2026 | Publisher: Pintar Indonesia

1. Introduction

Pintar Indonesia ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

2. Data We Collect

To provide our core public speaking evaluation service, we collect the following types of data:

  • Audio Data: When you use the recording feature, the App captures your voice.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect basic profile information such as your email address and name.
  • Usage Data: We collect basic analytics on how you interact with the App (e.g., features used, session length) to improve user experience.

3. How We Process Audio and Use AI

The core functionality of this App relies on Artificial Intelligence.

  • Your recorded audio is transmitted securely to third-party AI service providers for transcription and structural analysis.
  • We do not use your personal audio recordings to train our own foundational AI models.
  • Audio files are processed ephemerally and are not permanently stored on our servers after the analysis is generated.

4. Data Retention and Local Storage

Your analysis results (transcripts and feedback metrics) are stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete this history.

5. Account and Data Deletion

You have the right to request the complete deletion of your account and associated data.

  • In-App Deletion: You can delete your account directly within the App by navigating to Settings > Account > Delete Account.
